INSTALLATION

The term "custom" implies that these tanks are tailored to the unique needs of a particular project / client. Custom factory-assembled tanks refer to tanks that are manufactured off-site, typically in our Precast Factory and then transported to the installation site as fully assembled units. These tanks are designed to meet specific requirements and can serve various purposes, including water storage, wastewater treatment, chemical storage, and more.

ADVANTAGE CUSTOM FACTORY ASSEMBLED TANKS

Can be designed in various shapes, sizes, and configurations to meet the specific needs and constraints of the clients.

Design considerations include capacity, dimensions, material specifications and special features.

Walls are cast in the factory using higher grade concrete, steel providing necessary accessories for connecting the Walls and also with the Base Slab. Walls are connected with the post tensioning method and the Base Slab is cast keeping the connected walls in position.

Necessary water proofing measures are undertaken during casting..

Manufacturing in the controlled factory environment allows for stringent quality control measures, ensuring that the tank meets industry standards and project specifications.

Depending on the application, custom tanks are made to be integrated with other systems such as plumbing, electrical, or monitoring systems as required by the clients.

Manufactured to meet the relevant codes and regulations, industry standards and regulatory requirements..

Can be used for a wide range of applications, including water storage, industrial processes, chemical containment, wastewater treatment, and more.

Maintenance requirements and expected lifespan of the tank are considered as per client’s needs.

While custom factory-assembled tanks may have little higher upfront costs, they provide cost savings in terms of reduced on-site construction time and potentially lower maintenance costs over the long term.

Suitable for projects where specific design considerations or environmental factors make off-site manufacturing a preferred option. They offer a tailored solution that meets the unique requirements of the application.
