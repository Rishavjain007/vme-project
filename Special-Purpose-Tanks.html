SPECIAL PURPOSE TANKS (To store other Liquids)

We manufacture Special Purpose Tanks of various capacities as per the clients’ requirements for storing liquids other than water used in various industrial, commercial, and agricultural applications. If the size is small or medium, the entire unit is precast at factory and transported to the site for erection.

If the size is bigger (i.e.) more than 45 KL, these tanks are constructed using the precast concrete panels which are cast at factory and then assembled on-site.

These tanks are designed based on the requirements of the clients. The materials, tanks size, panel size, materials to be used, method of execution etc. depend on the purpose of usages viz. wastewater treatment, ETP, chemical storage, Septic tanks, oil storage, biogas storage, acid storage, fertilizer storage, etc.

The specific requirements of the stored substances are considered and relevant safety and environmental regulations are complied with when designing the precast concrete tanks for storing liquids other than water. The design and construction of these tanks are tailored to the properties of the liquids to ensure containment and prevent any potential hazards, adhering to industry standards.
