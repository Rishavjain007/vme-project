PRECAST TANKS FOR WASTEWATER TREATMENT

BE SMART, USE PRECAST

We offer an extensive range of Precast Monolithic concrete tanks of desired sizes and shapes suitable for all unit operations and processes of industrial and domestic wastewater treatment

The various tanks used for the treatment process:

  • Collection Tank / Equalization Tank
  • Aeration tank
  • Settling Tank
  • Clarifier tank
  • Treated Water Tank

These tanks can be combined into one single tank or a combination of tanks as illustrated below

FEATURES

  • Monolithically Cast
  • Durable and lasts long
  • No fear of leaking
  • Leakage Test done at factory
  • Water Proofing Material applied after leakage testing
  • Inlet and outlet provisions given in the tank
  • Partition walls can be fixed based on volume requirement of each chamber
  • In case of handling hazardous waste, special coating will be given
  • No uplift of tanks in high water table areas or when tank is empty
  • For settling chamber, bottom slope is given
  • Reinforced Cement Concrete cover will be provided
  • If tanks are placed underground, LMV or HMV can move over it
  • The above features applied for rectangular tanks only
