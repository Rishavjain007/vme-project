SMALL DOMESTIC TANKS

Small Domestic Tanks are produced off-site (i.e.) at the factory and then transported to the installation location, offering advantages such as quick installation, durability, and ease of maintenance.

Available in various capacities of 500 litres, 1000 litres, 1500 litres and 2000 litres.

Manufactured as a single unit with cover slab or without cover slab as per the requirement of the clients.

As an another method of production, Base slabs, Rings and Cover Slabs are manufactured separately and supplied to the clients to fix them at site with leak proof rubber gasket joints with the help of trained plumbers.

Can be installed anywhere like on ground, underground and roof tops.

Necessary provisions have been made for plumbing and other connections.

Mainly used for various purposes, such as water storage, septic systems, stormwater management, or wastewater treatment.

Each and every piece is tested for water leakage before clearing for the delivery.

Inner Water Proof Coating done using a mixture of Nitto Bond, Cement and Polymers. On outer surface a damp proof coating is done.
